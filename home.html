Instructions

Once you choose your gender, you can start selecting your body shape and then simulate verious clothing. Any piece you select has multiple sizes, which you can switch between. You can also choose the color, by giving the RGB input. If you can see holes in your chosen clothing, we can assume it doesn't fit the model and you should adjust the size.

Material

First you should start a simulation with stiff material, which tries to keep the shape of original model. If the cloth piece does not adapt well enough to the body shape, you will want to try use the soft material. Soft material can also be used, not only for better adaptation, but just different behaviour under gravity. Also if pants waist does not hug the body well enough, you should switch to soft material for better adaptation.

WARNING: soft material can cause model shape to deform.
